Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

Using welcoming and inclusive language

Being respectful of different viewpoints

Accepting constructive criticism

Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

Harassing comments

Trolling or insulting other contributors

Publishing private information

Any conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate

Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing the standards and taking appropriate action in cases of unacceptable behavior.

Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces and when someone is representing the project in public spaces.

Enforcement

Instances of abusive or inappropriate behavior may be reported to the maintainers at [email/contact here]. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
